CacheCoin (CACHE)

Whitepaper Lite — Version 0.1

What is CacheCoin?

CacheCoin is a community token for geocachers. It adds an optional digital tipping layer to geocaching — a simple way to say “thanks” for great hides, creative containers, and well-maintained caches.

CacheCoin does not replace geocaching. Participation is optional.

Why CacheCoin Exists

Geocaching thrives on effort and creativity, but recognition is usually limited to logs and favorites. CacheCoin explores whether small, optional rewards can encourage creativity, thank cache owners, and support community events.

How CacheCoin Is Used

1. Wallet-to-Wallet Tips

Geocachers with a Solana wallet can receive CacheCoin directly via wallet address or QR code.

2. Claim-Later Rewards

Some caches may include a sealed claim code. Finders can redeem later after setting up a wallet, keeping participation accessible.

Core Values

  • Fun first: the hunt comes before the token.
  • Transparency: one real mint, public links.
  • Respect: independent and community-led.
  • Accessibility: optional, low-friction participation.

What CacheCoin Is NOT

  • Not an investment
  • Not a promise of profit
  • Not required to geocache
  • Not affiliated with Geocaching®
